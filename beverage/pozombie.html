Pine-Orange Zombie

Cooking Tools Needed:

  1. Blender
  2. Glasses
  3. Measuring Spoons

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cups Brandy
  2. 2 Grenadine Syrup
  3. 2 White Rum
  4. 2 Dark Rum
  5. 2 Lime Juice
  6. 4 cup Ice Cubes
  7. 1 can (240 ml) Del Monte
  8. 100% Pineapple Juice with ACE (chilled)
  9. 1 can (240 ml) Del Monte
  10. Sweetened Orange Juice Drink (chilled)

Procedure:

  1. Blend all ingredients with ice in a blender.
  2. Pour into a glasses.
  3. Garnish with desired fruit slice, sprig of mint and a cherry.

Makes 3 servings

Lusog Notes: This drink is rich in vitamin C, which helps fight off common infections.
