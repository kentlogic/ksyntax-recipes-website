Green Kale Lemonade

Ingredients:

  1. 1 bunch kale (use stalks and all)
  2. 1 cucumber (I leave the skin on)
  3. 1 lemon (I leave the peel on)
  4. 1 Granny Smith apple (remove core)

Procedure:

  1. Juice all ingredients
  2. Place green lemonade in glasses
  3. Serve and Enjoy!

Makes 1-2 servings
