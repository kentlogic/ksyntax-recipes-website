Pine-Orange Mai Tai

Cooking Tools Needed:

  1. Glasses
  2. Measuring Cups
  3. Measuring Spoons
  4. Shaker

Ingredients:

  1. 2 Light Rum
  2. 1-1/2Triple Sec
  3. 2 Lime Juice
  4. 1/2 tsp Grenadine Syrup
  5. 1 tbsp Dark Rum
  6. 2 cup Ice Cubes
  7. Maraschino Cherry
  8. 1 can (240 ml) Del Monte
  9. Sweetened Pineapple Juice Drink

Procedure:

  1. Pour all the ingredients into a shaker with ice cubes.
  2. Shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass half filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with cherry.

Makes 1-2 servings

Lusog Notes: This cocktail is rich in immunity-boosting vitamin C.
