Four Seasons Float (Single)

Cooking Tools Needed:

  1. Glasses
  2. Measuring Cups
  3. Measuring Spoons

Ingredients

  1. ¼ cup Clear Soda
  2. 1 scoop Strawberry Ice Cream
  3. 1 can (240 ml) Del Monte
  4. Four Seasons Juice Drink (chilled)

Procedure:

  1. Pour DEL MONTE Four Seasons Juice Drink in glasses.
  2. Add clear soda. Top with scoop of ice cream just before serving.
  3. Serve and Enjoy!

Makes 1 serving
Lusog Notes: Keep your child’s immune system healthy with this vitamin C-rich treat.
