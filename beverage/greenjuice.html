Simple Green Juice

Cooking Tools Needed:

  1. Juicer

Ingredients

  1. 1 bunch parsely
  2. 2 cucumbers (I leave the peel on)
  3. 1 lemon (remove yellow part of skin)

Procedure:

  1. Push parsley, cucumbers and lemon through a juicer
  2. Serve and Enjoy!

Tip:
Remove the yellow part of the lemon skin because it can be very bitter when juiced. Leave the white part of the skin on as it is full of healthy bioflavonoids. This tangy, refreshing juice is great with breakfast, as an afternoon pick-me-up, or any other time of day.
