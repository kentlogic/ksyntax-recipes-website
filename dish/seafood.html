Seafood Soup Sweet and Sour

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 tbsp Patis
  2. 1 to 2 tbsp Sinigang Mix Powder
  3. 3 pc Shallots (sliced)
  4. 1 stalk Lemon Grass/Tanglad
  5. 1 tbsp Ginger (strips)
  6. 1/2 cup Canned Mushroom (button/straw/enoki/oyster, sliced)
  7. 200 grams Fish Fillet (cut into chunks)
  8. 200 grams (medium) Shrimps (peeled with tails intact)
  9. 150 grams Squid (ink sac removed)
  10. 1 pc Siling Haba
  11. 3 stalks Wansuy (sliced)
  12. 1 pack (57 grams) Del Monte
  13. Quick 'n Easy Sweet and Sour Mix

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Boil 2 cups water with DEL MONTE Quick 'n Easy Sweet & Sour Mix, patis, tamarind mix, lemon grass, ginger and mushrooms.
  2. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Add fish shrimps, squid and sili. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add with wansuy. Serve.

Makes 6 servings

Lusog Notes: This seafood dish is rich in vitamin B12, which helps prevent anemia.
