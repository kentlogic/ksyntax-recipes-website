Noodle Soup with All the Trimmings
( Batchoy )

Ingredients:

  1. 8 cups (2 liters) water
  2. 1/3 lb (150 g) bone-in or 1/4 lb (125 g) boneless pork butt or loin
  3. 1/4 lb (125 g) pork or chicken liver
  4. 1 chicken breast, preferably bone-in
  5. 2 tablespoons oil
  6. 3 cloves garlic, crushed with the side of knife and minced
  7. 1 onion, finely sliced
  8. One 2-in (5-cm) piece ginger, peeled and finely sliced
  9. 1/4 lb (125 g) fresh shrimp, shelled and deveined
  10. 1 teaspoon salt
  11. 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  12. 1 tablespoon bottled sauteed shrimp paste (page 16)
  13. 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  14. 8 oz (240 g) fresh Chinese egg noodles or fettucine or 4 oz (100 g) dried ramen noodles
  15. 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Garnishes

  1. 4 green onions (scallions), finely sliced
  2. 4 tablespoons Fried Garlic
  3. 1/2 cup (70 g) fried pork rinds, coarsely crushed

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a stockpot.
  2. Add the pork, liver, and chicken then cook over high heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Skim off the fat.
  4. Using a slotted spoon, remove the pork, chicken, and liver.
  5. Reserve the broth.
  6. Shred the meat using two forks—one to hold the meat secure on a cutting board and the other to pull the meat into shreds.
  7. Slice the liver thinly. Set aside the shredded meat and sliced liver.
  8. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  9. Add the garlic and saute until lightly browned.
  10. Add the onion and saute until translucent.
  11. Add the ginger and saute for 3 minutes.
  12. Turn off the heat and carefully remove them from the skillet, keeping as much oil in the skillet as possible, and set aside.
  13. In the same skillet, saute the shredded pork and chicken over medium heat for about 5 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  14. Add the shrimp and saute for 5 minutes more. Set aside.
  15. Add the sauteed garlic and ginger to the broth in the stockpot.
  16. Bring to a boil and add the salt, fish sauce, sauteed shrimp paste and sugar.
  17. Reduce the heat to medium and add the noodles.
  18. Simmer until the noodles are soft— about 10 minutes for dried noodles and 2 to 3 minutes for fresh noodles.
  19. Add the pepper.
  20. Ladle the broth and noodles into individual bowls.
  21. Top with the sauteed pork and chicken and sliced liver.
  22. Sprinkle on the scallions, fried garlic and fried pork rinds.
  23. Serve hot.

Makes 4-6 servings

Preparation time: 30 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes
