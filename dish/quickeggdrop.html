Quick Chicken Eggdrop Soup
( Misuang Manok )

Ingredients:

  1. 1 tablespoon oil
  2. 5 cloves garlic, crushed with the side of knife and minced
  3. 1 onion, sliced thinly
  4. 8 cups (2 liters) water
  5. 1 chicken breast or thigh, preferably bone-in, sliced
  6. 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  9. 2 eggs, beaten
  10. 4 oz (100 g) dried white wheat noodles (misua)
  11. 3 green onions (scallions), minced

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and sauté the garlic until lightly browned.
  2. Add the onion and sauté until translucent. Add the water and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the chicken, fish sauce, salt and pepper and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the egg slowly while stirring the soup.
  5. Add the noodles and simmer over medium-low heat for 3 minutes, or until soft.
  6. Ladle the soup into individual bowls and garnish with the green onions.
  7. Serve immediately.

Makes servings

Preparation time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 30 minutes
