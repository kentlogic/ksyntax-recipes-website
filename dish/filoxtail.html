Filipino Oxtail Stew

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 pounds beef oxtail, cut into pieces
  2. 1 large onion, quartered
  3. 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  6. 1 large eggplant, cut into 2-inch chunks
  7. 1/2 head bok choy, cut into 1-inch pieces
  8. 1/2 pound fresh green beans, trimmed and snapped into 2-inch pieces
  9. 1/4 cup peanut butter, or as needed to thicken sauce

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Fill a large saucepan with water, and drop in the oxtail pieces, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring to a boil, and simmer for 2 hours over medium-low heat, skimming the foam occasionally, until the oxtail meat is very tender and the broth is reduced to 3 cups.
  3. Stir in the eggplant, bok choy, and green beans, and simmer for about 20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Just before serving, place the peanut butter in a small bowl and thin with 1 or 2 tablespoons of broth.
  5. Stir until smooth and add to the stew.

Serve and Enjoy!
