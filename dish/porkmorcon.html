Pork Morcon

Ingredients

  1. 3 kg Pork Lomo (Tenderloin) (sliced morcon style)
  2. 6 tbsp Calamansi Juice
  3. 6 tbsp Soy Sauce
  4. 3/4 tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  5. 6 pc (small) Eggs (hard boiled, shelled and sliced into 4 wedges, for stuffing)
  6. 6 pc Chorizo de Bilbao (cut into 4 strips, for stuffing)
  7. 408 grams Bacon (for stuffing)
  8. 300 grams Carrots (cut into 3 strips, for stuffing)
  9. 6 pc (whole) Pickles (cut into 3 strips, for stuffing)
  10. 300 grams Cheddar Cheese (cut into 4 strips, for stuffing)
  11. 6 pc (small) Red Bell Pepper (cut into 4 strips, for stuffing)
  12. 6 pouches (80 grams each) Del Monte
  13. Quick 'n Easy Mechado Sauce

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Pound pork to flatten. Sprinkle with calamansi juice and soy sauce.
  2. Starting on one end of the meat, arrange in alternate rows the stuffing until all are used up.
  3. Roll and tie with a string (crocheting thread).
  4. Sprinkle with flour then brown all sides in 2 tablespoons oil.
  5. Transfer meat into saucepan, then add DEL MONTE Mechado Sauce and 71/2 cups water.
  6. Cover and Simmer over low heat for 1 hour or until tender. Remove strings and slice.

Makes 30 servings
Lusog Notes: This is rich in vitamin B1, for healthy brain and nerve function.
