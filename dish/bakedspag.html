Baked Spaghetti with Meat

Ingredients

  1. 2 tbsp Margarine
  2. 4 cloves Garlic (crushed)
  3. 1 pc (medium) Onion (chopped)
  4. 100 grams Ground Beef (you can use Ground Pork)
  5. 1 pc Chicken Bouillon Cube
  6. 1 pc (medium) Red Bell Pepper (diced)
  7. 2 pc Eggs (beaten)
  8. 1 cup Melting Cheese (coarsely grated )
  9. 200 grams Del Monte
  10. Spaghetti (cooked)
  11. 1 pouch (250 grams) Del Monte
  12. Sweet Style Spaghetti Sauce

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven at 3750F. Grease a 7"x7" pan.
  2. Saut√© garlic, onion, pork and bouillon cube in margarine.
  3. Add Del Monte Sweet Style Spaghetti Sauce, bell peppers.
  4. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix with cooked DEL MONTE Spaghetti and half of cheese.
  6. Arrange in baking pan.
  7. Pour eggs on top. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Let stand at room temperature for 5 minutes.

Makes 4 servings.
