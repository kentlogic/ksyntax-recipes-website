Vegetable Lumpia in Kare-Kare Sauce

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pc (small) Onion (sliced)
  2. 150 grams Ground Pork
  3. 150 grams Sitaw (cut into rings)
  4. 150 grams Banana Heart (trimmed and sliced)
  5. 1 1/2 tsp Patis
  6. 150 grams Cabbage (sliced)
  7. 15 pc Lumpia Wrappers (medium round )
  8. 1/2 tbsp Soy Sauce (for sauce)
  9. 4 cloves Garlic (minced, for sauce)
  10. 2 1/2 tbsp Brown Sugar (for sauce)
  11. 1 pack (50 grams) Del Monte
  12. Quick 'n Easy Kare-Kare Mix

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Saute onion and pork for 3 minutes. Add sitaw, banana heart and patis.
  2. Cook for 5 minutes with occasional stirring.
  3. Add 2 tbsps of DEL MONTE Quick n Easy Kare-kare Mix.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes. Add cabbage. Stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Set aside.
  6. Combine 2/3 cup water, sauce ingredients and remaining kare kare powder.
  7. Stir to dissolve.
  8. Simmer with continuous stirring until thick.
  9. Set aside.
  10. Wrap every 2 tablespoons of vegetable mixture in lumpia wrapper.
  11. Roll and steam for 1 minute. Pour sauce just before serving.

    12. Makes 5 servings (3 pcs per serving with 2 tablespoons sauce)

    Serve and Enjoy!

    Lusog Notes: Fight the common cold with this dish, which is rich in vitamin C.
