Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  1. 2 1/2 lbs (1.25 kg) bone-in chicken breasts, thighs or drumsticks
  2. 3 cloves garlic, crushed with the side of knife
  3. 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  4. 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  5. 1 bay leaf
  6. 1 large onion, sliced into rings

    7. Adobo Marinade

  7. 1 cup (250 ml) Filipino cane vinegar
  8. 1/4 cup (65 ml) freshly squeezed calamansi or lime juice
  9. 1 cup (250 ml) soy sauce

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Combine the Marinade ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix thoroughly.
  2. Add the chicken and store overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Remove the chicken from the Marinade. Reserve the Marinade.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the oil.
  5. Add the garlic and saute until lightly browned.
  6. Add the chicken and saute for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the Marinade, peppercorns, sugar, and bay leaf.
  8. Cover the pan and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce the heat to medium-low.
  10. Simmer until the meat is tender, 45 minutes or more.
  11. Add the onion and simmer 5 minutes more.
  12. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Preparation time: 5 minutes + overnight marinating time
Cooking time: 1 hour
