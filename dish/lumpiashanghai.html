Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  1. 300 grams large Galunggong (Filleted and meat chopped)
  2. 1 tsp Calamansi Juice
  3. 1 stalk Kinchay (chopped)
  4. 1/2 pc (50 grams) Carrots (chopped)
  5. 1/2 pc (50 grams) Singkamas (chopped)
  6. 1/2 pc Pork Bouillon Cube
  7. 1 pc (small) Onion
  8. 6 tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  9. 15 pc (small) Lumpia Wrappers
  10. 1 pouch (90 grams) Del Monte
  11. Filipino Style Tomato Sauce

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Scrape flesh of fish with a spoon. Remove bones.
  2. Mix with calamansi juice, 1-1/4 tsp fine salt, 1/4 tsp pepper and kinchay.
  3. Combine with remaining ingredients except lumpia wrappers.
  4. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Wrap every tablespoon of mixture in lumpia wrapper.
  6. Fry until golden brown. Serve with DEL MONTE Ketchup.

Makes 5 servings

Lusog Notes: High in vitamin B1 prevents Beri-Beri.
