Fruits for the Gods

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/3 cups All-Purpose Flour (sifted)
  • 3/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Iodized Fine Salt
  • 1 cup Raisins
  • 3/4 Butter
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 3 pc Egg
  • 1 cup (+2 tbsp) Toasted Cashew Nuts (chopped)
  • 1 can (432 grams) Del Monte
  • Fiesta Fruit Cocktail (drained for 2 to 3 hours)

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF. Grease a 13”x9”x2” pan.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt together.
  3. Take 1/2 cup of this mixture and mix with DEL MONTE Fiesta Fruit Cocktail and raisins. Set aside.
  4. Cream butter until soft and fluffy. Add sugars and blend well.
  5. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Fold in the flour mixture.
  6. Add cashew and fruit cocktail mixture. Stir to completely blend batter.
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 10 minutes. Lower heat to 300ºF and continue baking for 50 minutes.
  8. Cool slightly then cut into bars.

Makes 24 servings
Lusog Notes: Fruits for the Gods contains calcium that helps build strong bones and teeth.
