Maja Blanca

Ingredients:

  • Change Servings
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh sweet corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup sweetened flaked coconut

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Butter an 8-inch baking dish or pie pan, and set aside.
  2. Mix 1/2 cup of water with the cornstarch in a bowl, and stir until smooth.
  3. Combine the coconut milk, 3/4 cup of water, and sugar in a saucepan over low heat, and stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, add the corn kernels, and then stir in the cornstarch mixture, stirring quickly to avoid lumps as it becomes very thick.
  5. Bring the mixture back to a boil, and simmer until fully thickened and smooth, stirring constantly, about 2 minutes.
  6. Pour the maja blanca into the prepared dish, and set aside to cool until firm, about 2 hours.
  7. Place the coconut flakes in a dry skillet over medium heat, and stir to toast. Watch them carefully so they don't burn.
  8. Remove the toasted coconut flakes to a bowl, let cool, and sprinkle over the pudding before serving.

Enjoy!
