Choco Mud Pies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Chocolate Chip Cookies (for crust)
  • 2 tbsp Margarine(for crust, melted)
  • 1 tbsp Sugar (for crust)
  • 1/2 tbsp Unflavored Gelatin(for cream filling, dissolved in reserved syrup )
  • 2 tbsp Evaporated Milk (for cream filling)
  • 1/2 cup Cream Cheese (for cream filling, softened)
  • 1 tbsp Sugar (for cream filling)
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Cream (for cream filling)
  • 1 can (432 grams) Del Monte Fiesta Fruit Cocktail (for cream filling, drained ,reserve 1 tbsp syrup,)

Cooking Procedure:

CRUST:

  • blend cookie crumbs, margarine and 1 tablespoon sugar.
  • Divide mixture into 6 cups or molds.
  • Chill until firm.

CREAM FILLING:

  1. Heat gelatine mixture and milk until gelatin is dissolved.
  2. Set aside eat cream cheese and 1 tablespoon sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add dissolved gelatin gradually while mixing until well mixed.
  4. Set aside whip cream for 2 minutes.
  5. Fold into gelatine mixture.
  6. Spread chocolate crust with 2 tablespoons of cream mixture then 2 tablespoons of DEL MONTE Fruit Cocktail.
  7. Top with remaining cream with cherry if desired.

    8. Makes 6 servings
