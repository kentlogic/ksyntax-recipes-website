Peanut Butter Protein Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 scoop Frank Zane Egg White Protein Powder (or protein powder of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter or creamy roasted almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 10 drops stevia
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Combine water, protein powder, peanut butter, vanilla and stevia in a vitamix
  2. Puree on high speed until smooth
  3. Blend in ice cubes
  4. Serve for breakfast or as a gluten free, dairy free snack

Enjoy!
