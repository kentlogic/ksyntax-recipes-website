Fruity Rainbow Sandwich

  • Bread Knife
  • Spreader
  • Chopping Board
  • Colander
  • Best paired with

Ingredients:

  • 12 pcs Sliced Bread
  • 3 tbsp Strawberry Jam (or raspberry jelly/jam spread)
  • 3 tbsp Peanut Butter Spread
  • 3 tbsp Orange Marmalade
  • 1 can (432 grams) Del Monte
  • Fiesta Fruit Cocktail (well drained)

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Remove crust of bread.
  2. Spread 3 alternate layers of bread with peanut butter, DEL MONTE Fiesta Fruit Cocktail, jam/jelly fruit cocktail and marmalade then fruit cocktail.
  3. Cover with another slice of bread.
  4. Cut sandwiches into 2 triangles. Toast if desired.
  5. garnish with remaining fruit cocktail if desired.

Makes 6 servings
