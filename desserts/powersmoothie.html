Breakfast Power Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • 1 tablespoon creamy roasted almond butter
  • 5 drops vanilla cr√®me stevia
  • 1 scoop Frank Zane Egg White Protein Powder (or protein powder of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds
  • 3 ice cubes

Cooking Procedure:

  1. Combine strawberries, coconut water, almond butter and stevia in a vitamix and blend on high
  2. Blend in protein powder and ground chia seeds, then blend in ice cubes until smooth
  3. Serve and Enjoy!
  4. Note: if your smoothie is too thick, blend in a little more coconut water
